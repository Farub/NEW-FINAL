WE ARE ECOBUILD

cost-effective solutions for the construction sector

where innovation meets sustainability in the construction industry. Founded by Rafeeq Fredericks, our mission is to turn construction waste into opportunities through our cutting-edge marketplace powered by AI and blockchain. Every year, South Africa generates millions of tons of construction waste, with only 10% being recycled. At EcoBuilt Connect, we are addressing this critical challenge by connecting contractors, recyclers, and suppliers on a single platform to trade eco-certified materials sustainably and affordably.
